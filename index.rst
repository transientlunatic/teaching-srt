Neutral hydrogen in the Galaxy
=============================

These are some notes on working with observations of neutral hydrogen in the Milky Way.
They're very much a work-in-progress at present, but they're designed to assist with HI-related lab projects conducted as part of the honours labs at the University of Glasgow.
